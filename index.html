Crochet @ Play

Welcome to Crochet At Play! Your one stop destination for absolutely free crochet patterns and tutorials. Looking for something in particular? Check out our Categories page to see free patterns by subject. Have you been here before? Visit our Recently Added page to find patterns added to the site within the last 21 days. Feeling overwhelmed? Use our search feature to find just what you're looking for in one easy step. Have a pattern you would like listed on our site? Visit our Submit page to send it our way. This site is updated daily, but that is only possible when we receive free patterns from crocheters just like you. Crochet At Play pages now feature page preview enhancements! Looking for just that certain pattern when nothing else will do and you don't want to spend time clicking on every link? Click on the "Visit Enhanced" page link found at the top of the Categories and Recently Added pages to add site preview to all the pages. Site preview allows you to preview the pattern page in a small pop up box before visiting. Pop up boxes are interactive, meaning you can scroll within the page, to switch to a new preview either move your cursor to a new link or click the close button at the bottom of the pop up.
